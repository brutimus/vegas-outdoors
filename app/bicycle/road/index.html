Road

Whether you clad yourself in spandex or hop on for a leisurely ride, the Las Vegas Valley is filled with road cycling opportunities. Take your pick from options such as the Red Rocks Loop, the River Mountains Loop or any number of city trails.

Groups

Sometimes cycling is just more fun with friends. If that's what you're looking for, take a look at some of the area's cycling groups.
