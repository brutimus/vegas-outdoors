Routes

Commuting on a bicycle in the Las Vegas Valley is rarely a straight forward endeavor. With no established bicycle routes through the city, daily commutes become a guessing game of where bike trails and lanes connect.

Here, we would like to establish a list of best routes in and around the city. With these established routes, we hope to encourage more people to hop on the bike rather than drive to the grocery store. Also, with more cyclists concentrated on a few popular routes, we hope to eventually garner civil support and see more resources concentrated on lane marking and signs.
