Routes

Bicycle commuting in Las Vegas is rarely straight forward. With no established bicycle routes through the city, daily commutes become a guessing game of where bike trails and lanes connect. Establishing a map of preferred routes will concentrate bicycle traffic on fewer streets to increase safety and allow more civil resources to be used on these travel corridors.
